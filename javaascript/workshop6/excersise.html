SessionStorage vs LocalStorage

  • localStorage keeps data even after browser is closed and reopened.
  • sessionStorage is cleared when the tab or browser window is closed.
  • Use localStorage for things like saved preferences or todo lists.
  • Use sessionStorage for temporary data during one session.
